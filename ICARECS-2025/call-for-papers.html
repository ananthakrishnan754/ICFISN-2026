Call for Papers

ICFISN 2025 invites full-length original research contributions from students, researchers, academicians, and industry professionals in the field of intelligent systems and next-gen networks. The conference aims to provide a platform for sharing innovative ideas and recent advances in the field.

Conference Topics

Submissions are invited in all areas of intelligent systems and next-gen networks, including but not limited to:

  • 5G and 6G Wireless Technologies
  • Artificial Intelligence in Communication Systems
  • MIMO and Massive MIMO Systems
  • Internet of Things (IoT) and Applications
  • VLSI Design and Embedded Systems
  • Optical Communication and Networks
  • Satellite and Space Communication
  • Signal and Image Processing
  • RF and Microwave Engineering
  • Antenna Design and Propagation
  • Wireless Sensor Networks
  • Network Security and Cryptography
  • Machine Learning for Communication
  • Green Communication Technologies
  • Quantum Communication
  • Smart Grid Communication
  • Biomedical Signal Processing
  • Nanoelectronics and Nanotechnology
  • Robotics and Automation
  • Edge Computing and Fog Networking

Authors are encouraged to submit original, unpublished research work that has not been submitted for publication elsewhere.
